About Us

At Cozy Paws at The Grand , our passion goes far beyond simply providing dog care — we treat every pup like a cherished member of our own family. What sets us apart is the level of love, detail, and comfort we put into every service. From personalized boarding experiences with snuggles and bedtime routines, to flexible memberships that give busy owners peace of mind, we’ve designed every aspect of our care around building trust and joy for both dogs and their humans. Unlike large, impersonal kennels, we focus on a warm, home-like environment where your dog feels safe, relaxed, and truly cared for, ensuring tails keep wagging long after they leave.

Terms, Policies & Insurance

Business Information

Cozy Paws at The Grand is operated by 4Four LLC (dba Cozy Paws at The Grand). We carry general liability and pet care coverage through Lemonade Insurance for property damage, injury, and pet‑related incidents occurring during scheduled services.

Service Agreement

  • All pets must be current on vaccinations and free of contagious illness/parasites.
  • Bookings are confirmed in writing (text/email/booking platform).
  • Disclose special needs, medications, or behavioral notes prior to service.

Payments

  • Payment is due at booking to reserve dates.
  • Accepted methods: card, digital wallet, or other methods as listed in your invoice.

Cancellation & Refunds

Walks, Daycare, Bathing, Drop‑Ins: Cancel 24 hours before your appointment for a full credit; less than 24 hours is non‑refundable.

Boarding: Cancel 7 days before start for a full credit; 3–6 days prior = 50% credit; within 48 hours is non‑refundable.

No‑Shows: Forfeit the booking fee.

Refunds: Issued only if Cozy Paws must cancel due to unforeseen circumstances. Credits are valid for 12 months.

Liability

  • We prioritize safety and care, but cannot be responsible for conditions beyond reasonable control.
  • In emergencies, we will contact you immediately and may transport your pet to the nearest veterinarian.
  • Pets are cared for at the owner’s risk.
  • Owners are financially responsible for any damages, repairs, or replacements caused by their pet that exceed $100 in value (including property, bedding/equipment, or other pets’ belongings).
